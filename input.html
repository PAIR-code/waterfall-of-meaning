These associations show how people use language. For example "caviar" and "pizza" aren't just two kinds of food--they have rich cultural associations. When a word crosses an axis in the display, its location shows where it falls on the spectrum - for instance, is "caviar" associated more with the word "cheap" or "expensive"?

To this end, any biases with these associations will be shown as well. For this reason, it's paramount to ensure that these models are not being used in ways that enforce existing biases, and to continue developing ways to de-bias these models.
